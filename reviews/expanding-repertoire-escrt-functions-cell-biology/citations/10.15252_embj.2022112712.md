# Excellence in life sciences publishing

EMBO Press champions excellence across the life sciences by publishing influential, open access research and commentary for a global audience. We lead in open science, ensuring transparency, data integrity, and reproducibility.

With professional scientific editors supporting authors at every step, we make publishing efficient, fair, and informed - sharing trusted discoveries worldwide and advancing science with the quality and credibility of the EMBO name.

## Latest updates

1.  Update

    ### [Highlight your published article with a video synopsis](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLXFKUjWnBvp7YMyxpfpvfoRnW9vSVEVYY)

    We invite authors to create short video animations that summarise key findings of their work as a way to further engage with the research community. We publish these ‘video synopses’ on our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLXFKUjWnBvp7YMyxpfpvfoRnW9vSVEVYY) and promote them on our journal social media accounts at the time of article publication. For more information please contact the editor who handled your manuscript.

     ![](https://media.springernature.com/w447h252/springer-cms-alt/rest/v1/content/27828548/data/v1?as=jpg)

2.  Update

    ### [Video calls with EMBO Press editors](https://forms.gle/fta7qjZNzUkeHA7e6)

    Our scientific editors are available for video calls at any time. Whether to discuss the suitability of a manuscript in development, receive advice on a manuscript that is ‘stuck’ at another journal or more generally about scientific publishing and life as an editor, our team is available. Submit your request and we’ll respond with available appointments.

     ![](https://media.springernature.com/w447h252/springer-cms-alt/rest/v1/content/27828546/data/v1?as=jpg)

3.  Update

    ### [Transparent finances - the costs of scientific publishing](https://www.embo.org/features/the-cost-of-scientific-publishing/)

    EMBO and EMBO Press are making their journals’ finances public to provide transparency and clarity about what it costs to publish articles in high quality, selective journals.

     ![](https://media.springernature.com/w447h252/springer-cms-alt/rest/v1/content/27828550/data/v1?as=jpg)
