# KRAS G12C

Glycine-to-cysteine substitution at codon 12 of KRAS. Long considered undruggable, KRAS(G12C) is
targetable by allele-specific covalent inhibitors that exploit the reactive cysteine to trap and
inactivate the mutant protein by controlling GTP affinity and effector interactions — cited as proof
that previously intractable oncogenic drivers can be drugged.
