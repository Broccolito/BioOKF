# Melanoma

Metastatic melanoma is a paradigm tumour for both targeted and immune therapy in the review.
BRAF-V600E melanoma is treated with RAF/MEK inhibitors, but acquires resistance through MAP-kinase
pathway reactivation identified by gain-of-function and CRISPR knockout screens. Melanoma is also a
leading responder to checkpoint blockade; acquired resistance to PD-1 blockade arises through JAK1/
JAK2 mutations and loss of beta-2-microglobulin, which impair interferon-gamma response and antigen
presentation.
