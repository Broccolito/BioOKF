# HER2-positive breast cancer

Breast cancer with amplification of the HER2 (ERBB2) receptor. The review highlights that the
combination of the synergistic anti-HER2 antibodies trastuzumab and pertuzumab markedly improves
survival, and that the modern HER2-targeted antibody-drug conjugate trastuzumab deruxtecan
(DS-8201a) shows durable efficacy even in HER2-low breast cancer, where HER2 amplification is below
the threshold for response to conventional anti-HER2 therapy.
