# PARP inhibitor

Pharmacologic class of drugs (e.g. olaparib, niraparib) that inhibit poly(ADP-ribose) polymerase.
The classical synthetic-lethal therapy: PARP inhibition kills BRCA-mutant cells whose homologous
recombination DNA-repair pathway is already defective. Maintenance PARP inhibition deepens responses
in BRCA-mutant ovarian cancer; reversion mutations in BRCA1/BRCA2 confer acquired resistance.
