# PI3K-AKT-mTOR signaling pathway

A core growth-signalling cascade (HER2-PI3K-AKT-mTOR) that is critical for both normal and cancer
cells and is heavily regulated by feedback. Targeting it almost always elicits adaptive responses:
mTOR or AKT inhibition relieves feedback suppression of receptor tyrosine kinases, and PI3Kalpha
inhibition in ER+ PIK3CA-mutant breast cancer triggers epigenetic reprogramming that increases ER
transcription. Subclonal mutations in PI3K-pathway genes (and PTEN loss driving PI3Kbeta signalling)
drive resistance to targeted therapy.
