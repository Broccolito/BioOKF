# RAS-RAF-MEK-ERK signaling pathway

The MAP kinase signalling cascade through which RAS, BRAF, MEK and ERK transmit proliferative
signals. Targeting it (RAF/MEK inhibitors) triggers profound ERK-dependent feedback relief, and
acquired resistance in BRAF-V600E melanoma converges on ERK-dependent, ERK-independent and
pathway-indifferent reactivation states identified by gain-of-function and CRISPR knockout screens.
