# Trastuzumab deruxtecan

A modern HER2-targeted antibody-drug conjugate (ADC): a monoclonal anti-HER2 antibody covalently
linked to a cytotoxic payload. It delivers a chemotherapy dose that would be far too toxic if given
systemically, directly to tumour cells. DS-8201a shows durable efficacy in HER2-low breast cancer,
demonstrating that a modern ADC can deliver chemotherapy based on low levels of target expression
even when the target is not an oncogene for that tumour.
