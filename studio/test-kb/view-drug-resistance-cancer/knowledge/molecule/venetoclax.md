# Venetoclax

BCL2 inhibitor and a unique example of a low-therapeutic-index agent: by lowering the apoptotic
threshold of cells it produces very deep responses in chronic lymphocytic leukaemia. Its on-target
liability — dramatic tumour lysis syndrome in patients with high disease burden — is managed with
careful dose escalation.
