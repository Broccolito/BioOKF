# ESR1

Gene encoding the oestrogen receptor (ER), a nuclear hormone receptor target in breast cancer.
Subclonal driver mutations in ESR1 — detectable in circulating tumour DNA — drive resistance to
endocrine therapy. Characteristic acquired mutations in the ligand-binding domain produce
ligand-independent (constitutive) transcription, a recurrent mechanism of hormone-resistant breast
cancer.
