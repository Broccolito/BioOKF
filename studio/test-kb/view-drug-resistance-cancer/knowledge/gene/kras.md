# KRAS

RAS-family GTPase oncogene, historically "undruggable" alongside MYC and TP53. Downstream KRAS- or
NRAS-activating mutations confer resistance to anti-EGFR antibody therapy in colorectal cancer
(target indifference). The allele-specific covalent inhibitor approach to the KRAS(G12C) mutant —
trapping and inactivating the mutant protein — is presented as a route to drugging a previously
intractable target.
