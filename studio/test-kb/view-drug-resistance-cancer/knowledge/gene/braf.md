# BRAF

Serine/threonine kinase oncogene in the RAS-RAF-MEK-ERK (MAP kinase) pathway. BRAF-V600E melanoma
responds to RAF/MEK inhibition but develops resistance through MAP-kinase pathway reactivation.
BRAF-mutant colorectal cancers are intrinsically insensitive to BRAF inhibitors because of feedback
reactivation of EGFR. Class 2 BRAF mutants signal as RAS-independent constitutive dimers that evade
ERK-dependent feedback and are only partially inhibited by existing allosteric MEK inhibitors.
