# BRCA1

DNA-repair (homologous recombination) tumour-suppressor gene; with BRCA2 it underlies the classical
synthetic-lethal vulnerability to PARP inhibition in BRCA-mutant tumours. Acquired resistance to
PARP inhibitors can occur through secondary reversion mutations in BRCA1 or BRCA2 that restore
DNA-repair function. Patients with germline BRCA mutations are flagged as a high-risk population in
which ctDNA screening may be appropriate.
