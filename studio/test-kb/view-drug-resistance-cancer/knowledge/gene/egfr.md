# EGFR

Receptor tyrosine kinase oncogene. Mutant EGFR drives a subset of NSCLC. Acquired resistance to
first-generation EGFR TKIs is dominated by the EGFR T790M gatekeeper mutation, overcome by the
third-generation irreversible inhibitor osimertinib. Resistance can also arise by MET amplification
(activating ERBB3 signalling). In BRAF-mutant colorectal cancer, feedback reactivation of EGFR
confers insensitivity to BRAF inhibition — a phenomenon largely absent in melanoma, which expresses
low EGFR.
