# Case 2025 fungal impacts on Earth's ecosystems review

Review by Nicola T. Case, Sarah J. Gurr, Matthew C. Fisher and colleagues (a large
multi-author consortium), *Nature* **638**, 49-57 (2025), "Fungal impacts on Earth's
ecosystems". The review surveys the fungal kingdom's role in Earth's ecosystems: nutrient
cycling and carbon sequestration, human-directed applications (food, beverages, biofuels,
pharmaceuticals), and the rising threat of fungal infections to crops, wildlife and humans,
amplified by human/animal movement and climate change. It also examines opportunities to
harness fungi against these threats.
