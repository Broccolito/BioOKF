# Bat population decline

Bat population decline refers to the mass mortality of hibernating bats observed across North
America. The review attributes much of this decline to white-nose syndrome caused by
*Pseudogymnoascus destructans*, citing it as a leading example of a fungal pathogen reshaping
wildlife populations and ecosystems.
