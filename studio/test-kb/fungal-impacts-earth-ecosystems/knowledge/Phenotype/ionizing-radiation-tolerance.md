# Ionizing radiation tolerance

Ionizing radiation tolerance is the capacity of some melanized fungi to withstand, and even
grow faster under, ionizing radiation. The review cites observations that ionizing radiation
changes the electronic properties of fungal melanin and enhances the growth of melanized
fungi, a trait of interest for biotechnological applications such as radiation-shielding
materials.
