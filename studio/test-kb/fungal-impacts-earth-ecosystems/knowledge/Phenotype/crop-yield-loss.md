# Crop yield loss

Crop yield loss is the reduction in agricultural harvest caused by disease, pests or abiotic
stress. The review highlights fungal crop pathogens — such as *Magnaporthe oryzae* (wheat and
rice blast) — as a major and growing cause of crop yield loss that threatens global food
security, urging coordinated action on fungal disease in crops.
