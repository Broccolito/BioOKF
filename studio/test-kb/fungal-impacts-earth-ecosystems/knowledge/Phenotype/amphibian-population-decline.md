# Amphibian population decline

Amphibian population decline refers to worldwide losses of frog, toad and salamander
populations. The review attributes a large share of these declines and extinctions to
chytridiomycosis caused by *Batrachochytrium* fungi, describing it as among the most
destructive wildlife diseases driven by a pathogen.
