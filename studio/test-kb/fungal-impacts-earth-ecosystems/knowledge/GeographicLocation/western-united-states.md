# Western United States

The arid and semi-arid western United States is the region endemic for *Coccidioides* and
coccidioidomycosis. The review cites longitudinal surveillance there showing that climate
variables (precipitation, heat, drought) drive the incidence and geographic expansion of
Valley fever.
