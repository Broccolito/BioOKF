# Pseudogymnoascus destructans

*Pseudogymnoascus destructans* is a psychrophilic fungus that causes white-nose syndrome in
hibernating bats, killing millions across North America. The review presents it as a
striking example of a cold-adapted fungal pathogen driving wildlife mortality, with spread
facilitated by human movement between bat hibernacula.
