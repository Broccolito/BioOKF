# Magnaporthe oryzae

*Magnaporthe oryzae* (*Pyricularia oryzae*) is the fungus that causes rice blast and the
emerging wheat blast disease. The review cites it among crop-destroying fungi that threaten
global food security, noting the growing urgency of fungal disease in crops and its
geographic spread aided by human movement and a changing climate.
