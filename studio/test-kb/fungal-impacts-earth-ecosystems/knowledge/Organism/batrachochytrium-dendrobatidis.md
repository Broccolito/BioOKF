# Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis

*Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis* (Bd) is a chytrid fungus that causes chytridiomycosis, a
disease responsible for catastrophic amphibian declines worldwide. The review uses Bd, and
its salamander-infecting relative *Batrachochytrium salamandrivorans*, as flagship examples
of fungal pathogens devastating wildlife — spread driven in part by human-mediated movement
of animals.
