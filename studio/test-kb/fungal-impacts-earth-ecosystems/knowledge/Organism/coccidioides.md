# Coccidioides

*Coccidioides* (species *C. immitis* and *C. posadasii*) is a dimorphic soil fungus endemic
to arid regions of the western USA and the Americas, and the cause of coccidioidomycosis
(Valley fever). The review highlights it as a climate-sensitive pathogen: precipitation,
heat and drought drive the incidence and geographic expansion of coccidioidomycosis, and it
has even been reported in southern sea otters, underscoring fungal threats to wildlife.
