# Aspergillus fumigatus

*Aspergillus fumigatus* is a ubiquitous saprotrophic mould and the principal agent of
invasive aspergillosis in humans. The review notes the worldwide emergence of azole
resistance in *A. fumigatus*, driven in part by agricultural azole use, as a threat to both
human health and food security. Invasive mould infections also followed environmental
disruption such as Hurricane Harvey.
