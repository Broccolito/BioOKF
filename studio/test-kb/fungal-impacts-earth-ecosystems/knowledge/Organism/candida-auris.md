# Candida auris

*Candida auris* is an emerging, frequently multidrug-resistant yeast pathogen of humans. It
is highlighted in the review as a paradigm of an emerging fungal threat whose spread and
azole/echinocandin resistance challenge human health. Its emergence has been linked to
climate-driven thermal adaptation, a recurring theme of the review.
