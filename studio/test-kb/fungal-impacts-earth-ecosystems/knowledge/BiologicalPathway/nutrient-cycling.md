# Nutrient cycling

Nutrient cycling is the movement and recycling of nutrients through decomposition. The review
underscores fungi as principal decomposers: by feeding on dead plants and animals, fungi
recycle nutrients and sequester carbon, making them indispensable to the functioning of
terrestrial ecosystems.
