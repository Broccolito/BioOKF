# Carbon sequestration

Carbon sequestration is the capture and long-term storage of carbon in ecosystems. The
review emphasizes that fungi, as heterotrophs feeding on almost any organic carbon, sequester
carbon into Earth's ecosystems while recycling nutrients through the decay of dead plants and
animals — a foundational ecosystem service of the fungal kingdom.
