# Azole antifungals

Azoles are the largest class of antifungal drugs and fungicides, inhibiting fungal ergosterol
biosynthesis. The review frames azoles as central to both human medicine and agriculture, and
identifies their dual use as a driver of the worldwide emergence of azole resistance in
pathogens such as *Aspergillus fumigatus* — a threat to human health and food security alike.
