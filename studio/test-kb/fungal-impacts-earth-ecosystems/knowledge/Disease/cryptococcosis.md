# Cryptococcosis

Cryptococcosis is infection by encapsulated *Cryptococcus* yeasts (*C. neoformans*,
*C. gattii*), ranging from pulmonary disease to life-threatening meningitis. The review
cites cryptococcosis among major human and wildlife fungal diseases, noting *C. gattii*
infections in marine mammals as evidence of fungal threats spreading across hosts.
