# Chytridiomycosis

Chytridiomycosis is a skin disease of amphibians caused by the chytrid fungi
*Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis* and *B. salamandrivorans*. The review cites it as the
single most devastating wildlife disease attributable to a pathogen, driving global
amphibian population declines and extinctions, with spread accelerated by the international
movement of amphibians.
