# Invasive aspergillosis

Invasive aspergillosis is a severe mould infection, most often caused by *Aspergillus
fumigatus*, affecting immunocompromised and critically ill patients. The review notes that
invasive mould infections surged after Hurricane Harvey and that azole resistance in
*A. fumigatus* complicates treatment, contributing to severe fungal disease incidence and
mortality.
