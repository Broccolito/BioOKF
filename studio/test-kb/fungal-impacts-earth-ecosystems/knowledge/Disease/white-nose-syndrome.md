# White-nose syndrome

White-nose syndrome is a fungal disease of hibernating bats caused by *Pseudogymnoascus
destructans*, characterized by white fungal growth on the muzzle and wings and massive
mortality. The review presents it as an emblematic wildlife fungal epizootic that has killed
millions of bats across North America, with spread linked to human movement between caves.
