# Wheat blast

Wheat blast is an emerging, fast-spreading crop disease caused by the *Magnaporthe oryzae*
(Triticum lineage) fungus, threatening global wheat production. The review uses it to argue
for addressing the growing urgency of fungal disease in crops, noting that fungal crop
pathogens jeopardize food security and that their range may expand with climate change.
