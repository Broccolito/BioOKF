# Candidiasis

Candidiasis is infection caused by *Candida* yeasts, including life-threatening invasive
disease (candidemia). The review discusses candidiasis — and emerging multidrug-resistant
agents such as *Candida auris* — as a major and growing human fungal disease burden.
