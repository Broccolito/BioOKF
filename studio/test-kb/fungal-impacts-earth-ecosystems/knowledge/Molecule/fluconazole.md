# Fluconazole

Fluconazole is a triazole antifungal that inhibits fungal ergosterol biosynthesis (lanosterol
14-alpha-demethylase). The review uses azoles such as fluconazole to illustrate both the
mainstay of antifungal therapy and the worldwide emergence of antifungal resistance, which is
amplified by the parallel agricultural use of azole fungicides.
