# Fungal melanin

Melanin is a pigment produced by many fungi that contributes to stress resistance and
virulence. The review notes functions of fungal melanin beyond virulence — including that
ionizing radiation changes the electronic properties of melanin and can enhance the growth
of melanized fungi, a property with potential biotechnological applications such as
radiation shielding.
