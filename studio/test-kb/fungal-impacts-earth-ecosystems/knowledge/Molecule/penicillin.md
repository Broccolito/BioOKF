# Penicillin

Penicillin is a beta-lactam antibiotic originally derived from *Penicillium* fungi. The
review cites penicillin as a landmark example of human-directed applications of fungi:
pharmaceuticals, including antibiotics, that have transformed medicine. It exemplifies the
beneficial side of the fungal kingdom that the review proposes harnessing further for the
bioeconomy.
