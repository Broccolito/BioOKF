# Amphotericin B

Amphotericin B is a polyene antifungal that binds ergosterol in fungal membranes. The review
discusses it among the limited classes of antifungal drugs available to treat severe human
fungal infections (alongside azoles and echinocandins), and frames the narrowness of the
antifungal arsenal as a key vulnerability against emerging, resistant fungal pathogens.
