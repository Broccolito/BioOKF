# Psilocybin

Psilocybin is a psychoactive tryptamine alkaloid produced by *Psilocybe* and related
mushrooms. The review lists psilocybin among the psychoactive compounds that exemplify
human-directed applications of fungi, alongside antibiotics and other pharmaceuticals,
highlighting the fungal kingdom as a source of bioactive natural products.
