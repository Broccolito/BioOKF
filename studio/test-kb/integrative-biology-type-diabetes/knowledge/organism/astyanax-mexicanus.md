# Astyanax mexicanus

The blind cave fish endures infrequent, limited nutrient availability and develops
hyperglycaemia, steatosis and [insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md) owing to a
mutation in its IR gene, similar to that seen in some patients with Rabson-Mendenhall syndrome.
Despite abnormal energy metabolism it shows delayed senescence, supporting a survival benefit of
limited insulin-dependent glucose disposal.
