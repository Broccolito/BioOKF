# Hepatic gluconeogenesis

Increased hepatic acetyl-CoA allosterically stimulates pyruvate carboxylase flux, which along
with increased glycerol flux maintains gluconeogenesis and endogenous glucose production. A single
oral saturated fat load was associated with 70% higher rates of hepatic gluconeogenesis. Fasting
[hyperglycaemia](../phenotype/hyperglycaemia.md) in T2D results from increased gluconeogenesis
plus hepatic insulin resistance.
