# De novo lipogenesis

Hepatic DNL is activated transcriptionally via SREBP1c, ChREBP, mTORC1-SREBP1c and
fructose-stimulated pathways, not exclusively via IR kinase activity. The review notes that NEFA
re-esterification, rather than DNL, probably accounts for the majority of hepatic lipogenesis and
VLDL secretion, contributing to [hepatic steatosis](../phenotype/hepatic-steatosis.md).
