# White adipose tissue lipolysis

Insulin inhibits lipolysis via ATGL/CGI-58, PDE3B and PKA-controlled hormone-sensitive lipase and
perilipins. In insulin-resistant states, immune-cell infiltration of dysfunctional
[WAT](../anatomy/white-adipose-tissue.md) increases lipolysis and NEFA/glycerol flux to the
[liver](../anatomy/liver.md), aggravating hepatic
[insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md) and NAFLD.
