# Physical exercise

A single bout of exercise activates AMP kinase, promotes translocation of
[GLUT4](../molecule/glut4.md) and glucose uptake independently of insulin, and completely
reversed the metabolic abnormalities (diversion of carbohydrate to hepatic DNL,
hypertriglyceridaemia) seen in insulin-resistant first-degree relatives of T2D patients.
