# Hyperglycaemia

Fasting hyperglycaemia in T2D results from increased rates of
[hepatic gluconeogenesis](../biologicalpathway/hepatic-gluconeogenesis.md) and endogenous glucose
production, together with hepatic insulin resistance that reduces insulin's ability to suppress
glucose output.
