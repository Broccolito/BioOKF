# Hepatic steatosis

The morphologic accumulation of [triacylglycerol/DAG](../molecule/diacylglycerol.md) in the
liver. The review notes that NEFA re-esterification (rather than de novo lipogenesis) probably
accounts for the majority of hepatic lipogenesis and VLDL secretion, and that weight loss from
very-low-calorie diets rapidly normalizes hepatic steatosis and hepatic insulin resistance.
