# Obesity disorder

Excess adiposity drives [white adipose tissue](../anatomy/white-adipose-tissue.md) dysfunction,
relative hypoxia and macrophage infiltration, which inhibit adipocyte insulin sensitivity and
increase lipolysis and NEFA flux to the liver, promoting hepatic
[insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md) and
[NAFLD](nafld.md).
