# Type 2 diabetes

A heterogeneous disease characterized by deficient insulin secretion by pancreatic islet
beta-cells in the context of [insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md). A GWAS
found more than 400 T2D-associated variants, mostly related to islet function, but the individual
genes explain less than 20% of overall disease risk. Most of the epidemic is driven by acquired
alterations, as shown by lifestyle-modification studies demonstrating T2D remission.
