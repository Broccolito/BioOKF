# 13C magnetic resonance spectroscopy

Direct assessment of glycogen synthesis by 13C MRS demonstrated lower rates of postprandial and
insulin-regulated hepatic glycogen synthesis in people with T2D, and quantified increased hepatic
[gluconeogenesis](../biologicalpathway/hepatic-gluconeogenesis.md). Combined with 31P MRS it
identified impaired insulin-stimulated muscle glycogen synthesis as the major factor in muscle
[insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md).
