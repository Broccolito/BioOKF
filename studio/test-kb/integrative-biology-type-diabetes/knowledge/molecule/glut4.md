# GLUT4

Inhibition of AS160 (TBC1D4) threonine phosphorylation and RAC1 activation accelerates
trafficking of GLUT4-containing storage vesicles to the plasma membrane, enabling insulin-
stimulated glucose uptake. A single bout of exercise promotes GLUT4 translocation and glucose
uptake independently of insulin via AMP kinase.
