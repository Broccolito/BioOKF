# White adipose tissue

Dysfunction of WAT and circulating metabolites initially modulate tissue communication and
insulin signalling. In insulin-resistant states, adipocyte dysfunction (relative hypoxia, HIF-1a
activation, macrophage infiltration) increases [lipolysis](../biologicalpathway/wat-lipolysis.md)
and NEFA/glycerol flux to the liver. [Leptin](../molecule/leptin.md) released from WAT signals
energy stores to the brain.
