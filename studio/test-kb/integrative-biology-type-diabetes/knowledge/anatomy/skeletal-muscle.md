# Skeletal muscle

13C and 31P magnetic resonance spectroscopy identified impaired insulin-stimulated glycogen
synthesis as the major factor responsible for muscle
[insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md), with reduced insulin-stimulated
[GLUT4](../molecule/glut4.md)-mediated glucose transport as the rate-controlling step. A single
bout of exercise activates AMP kinase and promotes GLUT4 translocation independently of insulin.
