# Liver

Increased fatty acid flux to the liver raises hepatic beta-oxidation and acetyl-CoA content,
which allosterically activates pyruvate carboxylase flux and maintains
[gluconeogenesis](../biologicalpathway/hepatic-gluconeogenesis.md). Hepatic
[DAG](../molecule/diacylglycerol.md) accumulation triggers
[PKCepsilon](../molecule/protein-kinase-c-epsilon.md) translocation and Thr1160 phosphorylation
of the insulin receptor, causing hepatic insulin resistance.
