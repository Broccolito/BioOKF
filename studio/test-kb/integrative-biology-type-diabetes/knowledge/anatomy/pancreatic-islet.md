# Pancreatic islet

The review attributes most of the 400+ T2D-associated gene variants to islet function. Initially,
[beta-cells](../celltype/pancreatic-beta-cell.md) compensate for insulin resistance by secreting
more insulin (hyperinsulinaemia); declining beta-cell function, partly due to glucolipotoxicity,
typically occurs just before T2D onset.
