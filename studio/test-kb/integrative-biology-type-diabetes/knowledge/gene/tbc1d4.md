# TBC1D4 (gene)

A common Greenlandic *TBC1D4* (AS160) variant confers muscle insulin resistance, postprandial
hyperinsulinaemia, hyperglycaemia and type 2 diabetes. AS160 links insulin signalling to
[GLUT4](../molecule/glut4.md) trafficking and glucose transport.
