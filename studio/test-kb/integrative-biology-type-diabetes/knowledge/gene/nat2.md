# NAT2 (gene)

A European GWAS reported that a nonsynonymous *NAT2* variant is associated with insulin
resistance, decreased adipocyte differentiation, decreased insulin-mediated glucose uptake and
increased WAT lipolysis. Silencing the mouse orthologue NAT1 induces
[insulin resistance](../phenotype/insulin-resistance.md), glucose intolerance, ectopic TAG/DAG
accumulation and activation of hepatic PKCepsilon and muscle PKCtheta.
