# TCF7L2 (gene)

In unbiased cluster analyses of recent-onset diabetes, a *TCF7L2* variant related to insulin
deficiency. Most of the 400+ T2D-associated variants relate to islet function but explain less
than 20% of overall [T2D](../disease/type-2-diabetes.md) risk.
