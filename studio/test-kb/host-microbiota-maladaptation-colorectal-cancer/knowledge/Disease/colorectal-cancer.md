# Colorectal cancer

Colorectal cancer (CRC) is a heterogeneous disease of the intestinal epithelium characterized by
the accumulation of mutations and a dysregulated immune response. Up to ~90% of disease risk is
attributed to environmental factors such as diet. A growing body of literature describes an
'oncogenic' CRC-associated microbiota; whether this dysbiosis is causal or a bystander effect
remains under investigation (Janney et al., Nature 2020).
