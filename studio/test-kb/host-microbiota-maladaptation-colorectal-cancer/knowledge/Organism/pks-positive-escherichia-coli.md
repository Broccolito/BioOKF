# pks+ Escherichia coli

Strains of *Escherichia coli* that carry the *pks* (polyketide synthase) genomic island and
produce the genotoxin colibactin. These bacteria induce DNA double-strand breaks and a specific
mutational signature in colonic epithelial cells, providing direct evidence of causality between
pks+ *E. coli* and established CRC driver mutations (Pleguezuelos-Manzano et al., Nature 2020,
as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020). Colibactin-producing *E. coli* promote colon tumour
growth, and small-molecule inhibitors can prevent these genotoxic and pro-tumoural effects.
