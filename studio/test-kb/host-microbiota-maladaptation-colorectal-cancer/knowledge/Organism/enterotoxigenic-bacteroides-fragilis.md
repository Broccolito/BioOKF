# Enterotoxigenic Bacteroides fragilis

Enterotoxigenic *Bacteroides fragilis* (ETBF) is a strain of *B. fragilis* that secretes the
*Bacteroides fragilis* toxin (BFT, fragilysin). ETBF drives a pro-carcinogenic inflammatory
cascade in the distal colon by inducing locally restricted chemokine expression and engaging the
IL-23/IL-17 inflammatory axis; polyamine catabolism contributes to ETBF-induced colon
tumorigenesis (Chung et al., Cell Host Microbe 2018; Goodwin et al., PNAS 2011, as reviewed by
Janney et al., Nature 2020).
