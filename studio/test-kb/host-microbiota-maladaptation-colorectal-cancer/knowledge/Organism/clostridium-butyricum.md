# Clostridium butyricum

*Clostridium butyricum* is a butyrate-producing commensal used as a probiotic. It inhibits
intestinal tumour development by modulating Wnt signalling and reshaping the gut microbiota,
illustrating a protective (anti-tumorigenic) member of the colonic community (Chen et al., Cancer
Lett. 2020, as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
