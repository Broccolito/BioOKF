# Janney 2020 host-microbiota maladaptation in CRC review

Review by Alina Janney, Fiona Powrie and Elizabeth H. Mann, *Nature* **585**, 509-517 (2020),
"Host-microbiota maladaptation in colorectal cancer". The review synthesizes host-microbiota
interactions in colorectal cancer (CRC), focusing on mechanisms linked to intestinal barrier
disruption, genotoxicity and deleterious inflammation. It notes that up to 90% of CRC risk is
attributed to environmental factors such as diet, consistent with an 'oncogenic' CRC-associated
microbiota whose causal vs. bystander role remains under investigation.
