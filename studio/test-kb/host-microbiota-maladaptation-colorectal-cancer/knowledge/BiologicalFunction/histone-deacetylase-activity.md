# Histone deacetylase activity

Histone deacetylase (HDAC) activity removes acetyl groups from histones, repressing transcription
and influencing colonocyte proliferation. The commensal metabolite butyrate is an HDAC inhibitor;
this inhibition underlies much of butyrate's regulation of intestinal macrophage function and its
anti-inflammatory, anti-carcinogenic effect in the colon (Chang et al., PNAS 2014, as reviewed by
Janney et al., Nature 2020).
