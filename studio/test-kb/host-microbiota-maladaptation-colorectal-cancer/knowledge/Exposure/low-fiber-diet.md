# Low-fiber diet

A low-fiber (Western-style) diet deprives the gut microbiota of fermentable substrate. The
fiber-starved community turns to foraging the colonic mucus layer, degrading the mucus barrier,
enhancing pathogen susceptibility and promoting aggressive colitis, thereby predisposing to
colorectal cancer (Desai et al., Cell 2016, as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
