# NF-kB signaling pathway

NF-κB is a master pro-inflammatory transcriptional pathway in colonic epithelial and immune cells.
*Bacteroides fragilis* toxin activates NF-κB to drive polarized CXC-chemokine (IL-8) and TGF-β
expression, linking microbial enterotoxins to the deleterious inflammation that promotes
colorectal cancer (Kim et al., Clin. Exp. Immunol. 2001, as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature
2020).
