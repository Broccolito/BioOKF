# IL-23/IL-17 inflammatory axis

The IL-23/IL-17 (Th17) axis is a pro-tumorigenic inflammatory programme triggered when adenoma
barrier defects allow microbial products to penetrate the epithelium. IL-23 from myeloid cells
drives IL-17-producing Th17 cells, fuelling tumour growth in colorectal cancer; enterotoxigenic
*B. fragilis* is a potent inducer of this axis (Grivennikov et al., Nature 2012, as reviewed by
Janney et al., Nature 2020).
