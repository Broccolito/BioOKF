# GPR109A

GPR109A (HCAR2) is the G-protein-coupled receptor for niacin and the commensal metabolite
butyrate. Activation of GPR109A on colonic epithelium and immune cells suppresses colonic
inflammation and carcinogenesis, mediating part of butyrate's anti-tumour effect (Singh et al.,
Immunity 2014, as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
