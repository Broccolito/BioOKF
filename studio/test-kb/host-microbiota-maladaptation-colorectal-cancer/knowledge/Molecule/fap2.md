# Fap2

Fap2 is a galactose-binding lectin/autotransporter of *Fusobacterium nucleatum*. By binding the
human inhibitory receptor TIGIT on natural killer and T cells, Fap2 suppresses immune cell
cytotoxicity and protects colorectal tumours from immune attack (Gur et al., Immunity 2015, as
reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
