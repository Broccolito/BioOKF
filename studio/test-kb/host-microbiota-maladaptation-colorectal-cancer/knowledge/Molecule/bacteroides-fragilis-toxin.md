# Bacteroides fragilis toxin

The *Bacteroides fragilis* toxin (BFT, fragilysin) is a zinc-metalloprotease secreted by
enterotoxigenic *B. fragilis*. By targeting colonic epithelial cells it cleaves E-cadherin and
activates NF-κB, driving polarized secretion of CXC chemokines (e.g. IL-8) and TGF-β and
coordinating a pro-carcinogenic inflammatory cascade in the distal colon (Kim et al., Clin. Exp.
Immunol. 2001; Sanfilippo et al., Clin. Exp. Immunol. 2000; Chung et al., Cell Host Microbe 2018,
as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
