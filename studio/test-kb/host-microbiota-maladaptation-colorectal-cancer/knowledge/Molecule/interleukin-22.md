# Interleukin-22

Interleukin-22 (IL-22) is a cytokine that acts on the intestinal epithelium to promote barrier
function and protect intestinal stem cells against genotoxic stress. Dietary stimuli mediate IL-22
signalling, which can shield stem cells from genotoxic insults; however, dysregulated IL-22 in the
context of chronic inflammation can also contribute to tumour growth, making its role
context-dependent (Gronke et al., Nature 2019; Perez et al., Nat. Commun. 2020, as reviewed by
Janney et al., Nature 2020).
