# Deoxycholic acid

Deoxycholic acid (DCA) is a secondary bile acid generated by gut bacteria from host primary bile
acids. It is co-mutagenic and genotoxic to colonic epithelial cells and is implicated as a
microbiota-derived promoter of colorectal carcinogenesis, linking high-fat diets to CRC risk
(Shibuya et al., Mutat. Res. 1997, as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
