# E-cadherin

E-cadherin (CDH1) is an epithelial cell-adhesion protein that normally sequesters β-catenin and
restrains Wnt signalling at colonic epithelial junctions. Microbial ligands target E-cadherin:
*Fusobacterium nucleatum* FadA binds E-cadherin to release β-catenin and activate Wnt signalling,
and *Bacteroides fragilis* toxin cleaves E-cadherin, both promoting epithelial proliferation in
colorectal cancer (as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
