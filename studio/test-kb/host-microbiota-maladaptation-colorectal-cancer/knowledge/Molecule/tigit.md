# TIGIT

TIGIT (T cell immunoreceptor with Ig and ITIM domains) is a human inhibitory immune-checkpoint
receptor on natural killer and T cells. *Fusobacterium nucleatum* Fap2 binds TIGIT to dampen
cytotoxic activity, allowing colorectal tumours to evade immune attack (Gur et al., Immunity 2015,
as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
