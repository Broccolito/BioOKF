# Colibactin

Colibactin is a genotoxic secondary metabolite produced by *pks*+ *Escherichia coli*. It
alkylates DNA and induces double-strand breaks, generating a distinctive mutational signature
linked to established CRC driver mutations and promoting colon tumour growth via a
senescence-associated secretory phenotype (Cougnoux et al., Gut 2014; Pleguezuelos-Manzano et al.,
Nature 2020). Small-molecule inhibitors of colibactin biosynthesis prevent its genotoxic and
pro-tumoural effects (Cougnoux et al., Gut 2016, as reviewed by Janney et al., Nature 2020).
